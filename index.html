Solar Subsidy – Gujarat

PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana (central + state)

💡 1-2 kW system: ₹30,000 subsidy

💡 2-3 kW system: ₹60,000 subsidy

💡 Above 3 kW: ₹78,000 fixed (for residential)

Gujarat state additional incentives for housing societies & MSMEs: up to ₹20,000 extra per unit.
